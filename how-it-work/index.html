How Stokkex Works

Stokkex makes buying and selling surplus industrial stock simple, transparent, and efficient. Follow these straightforward steps to get started.

1
List or Search Surplus Stock

Sellers list surplus or unused industrial equipment with detailed specifications, quantities, and availability. Buyers can search or browse verified listings to find exactly what they need — without long lead times.

2
Get Matched with the Right Partner

Stokkex connects verified buyers and sellers directly. All listings are reviewed to ensure accuracy, helping both parties engage with confidence.

3
Request a Quote or Make an Offer

Buyers can request pricing or submit offers directly through the platform. Sellers respond quickly, keeping negotiations clear and efficient.

4
Secure the Transaction

Once terms are agreed, Stokkex supports a secure transaction process, ensuring transparency and reliability for both sides.

5
Delivery & Completion

The stock is dispatched according to the agreed terms. Both parties complete the transaction knowing surplus stock has been put to productive use.
