About Stokkex

  • What is Stokkex?

    Stokkex is a B2B marketplace connecting buyers and sellers of surplus, over-ordered, unused, refurbished and project-specific specia list equipment across renewables, construction, infrastructure and industrial sectors.

    We help businesses unlock trapped inventory and source hard-to-find equipment faster.

  • Who can use Stokkex?

    Stokkex is exclusively for businesses.

    Users must be operating as a registered company, contractor, OEM, distributor or industrial organisation.

    We do not support consumer (B2C) transactions.

  • What types of equipment can be listed?

    Examples include (but are not limited to):

    • - Transformers
    • - Switchgear (LV/HV)
    • - Cable and cable accessories
    • - Battery storage systems (BESS components)
    • - Solar equipment
    • - EV charging equipment
    • - Generators
    • - Substation equipment
    • - Control panels
    • - Mechanical & M&E equipment

    All listings must be lawful, owned by the seller, and accurately described.

  • Is equipment new or used?

    Listings may include:

    • - New (unused / surplus)
    • - Over-ordered
    • - Cancelled project stock
    • - Refurbished
    • - Used (clearly stated condition)

    Sellers must clearly state the condition and provide accurate details.

  • Does Stokkex hold inventory?

    No.

    Stokkex is a marketplace platform only. We do not store, inspect, or take ownership of equipment.

    Transactions are conducted directly between buyer and seller.

  • How does payment work?

    Payment terms are agreed directly between buyer and seller unless otherwise specified through platform tools.

    Stokkex may introduce escrow or secure payment features in future updates.

  • Does Stokkex verify sellers?

    We conduct basic business verification checks, including:

    • - Companies House verification (UK where applicable)
    • - VAT number validation (where provided)
    • - Business identity screening

    However, buyers must conduct their own due diligence before completing transactions.

  • What fees does Stokkex charge?

    Stokkex operates on a membership and/or transaction fee model (depending on seller tier).

    Full pricing details are available in your account dashboard.

  • Who arranges transport?

    Logistics are agreed between buyer and seller.

    Stokkex may introduce logistics partnerships in future phases.

  • Can I list internationally?

    Yes.

    Stokkex supports cross-border listings where compliant with export regulations.

    Sellers are responsible for ensuring compliance with export controls and regulations.

  • What happens if there is a dispute?

    Disputes are handled between buyer and seller directly. Stokkex may assist in communication but is not liable for contractual disputes.

  • How do I join before launch?

    Register your interest via our Early Access form to be notified when the platform goes live.
